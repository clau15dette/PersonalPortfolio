About Me

I was born and raised in the seaside town of Southport, North Carolina. When I was seven years old, my father turned into Old MacDonald and bought a farm. Farm life was fun but also hard work. My parents instilled strong work ethics into me and my siblings at a very young age. They trained us to rise early, to work hard and to be honest.

My interests include international travel, reading, writing, learning foreign languages and jewelry designing. I also teach ESL and I am a published freelance author of "Southport."

Currently, I am a student at Coder Foundry, a Dot Net Boot Camp located in Kernersville, NC. This is an 18 week class that will conclude September 30, 2016. Upon graduation I will be a Jr. Full Stack Developer. I am here because I am reinventing my career. I have years of experience in accounts receivables/payables, mortgage insurance/bankruptcy, and customer service.

My plans are to return to Cary, NC and obtain a position as a Developer in one of the many companies there. My portfolio is in the process of being developed so each week you will be able to see changes in this site.
